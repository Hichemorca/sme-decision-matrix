How the Decision Engine Works
A transparent, rules-based model — not a black box.

50+ calibrated questions covering financial health, operations, market dynamics, pivot potential, and exit readiness.

Every answer maps to a Continue Score and an Exit Score, weighted by question importance and pillar weight.

The engine checks for conflicting answers. Each flag reduces Signal Strength.
